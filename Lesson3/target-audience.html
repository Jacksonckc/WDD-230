    Description of the target audience:

    To those why wish to be prepared for the upcoming weather, making sure that everyone brings a jacket when the wild weather puts some seasoning on the land where people live!

    Two Personas:

    1. Groups use seasoning to check the weather to determine their exercise plans. (Sports lovers)

    2. Groups use seasoning to prepare for incoming undesirable weathers, making sure that the event they host can go on on a certain day. (Event organizers/Organizations)

    Five Scenarios:

    1. Jenna wants to go out for a run in a park which is 5 miles next to her house, she puts on her shoes and gets ready for a good sunny-day run. Yet when she is halfway to the park, she finds that rain just came down. She wishes to know this before she comes out so she doesn't waste the time to changea and be disappointed.

    2. Joseph organizes an important event for the senator, he plans every detail out and checks all the equipment again and again before the event starts. However, the rain pours an hour before the start of the event, so Joseph has to reschedule everything, the senator is not happy. Joseph wishes that he had checked the weather before the event.

    3. Sue finally was able to ask for a day off so that she can go to the beach with her family. They have been planning this vaccation for quite a while. The day before the vaccation to Florida, a cold wind hit Florida and the temperature dropped significantly. Sue and her family ended up staying in the hotel and left the next morning. Sue wished that she had watched the news and check the weather before asking for a day off.

    4. Jose wants to propose to her girlfriend this Sunday on a bridge, he has seriously thought about it and decided it would be good for both of them. He wanted to take some pictures and film some videos to post on Instagram later. When he proposed, the weather was cloudy and dark, all the pictures and videos he took look bad and blury. He wishes it was brighter so the pictures and videos could come out beautiful.

    5. Talia was going to an important interview, she dressed up and put on pretty makeup. However, it was a humid day and all her makeup was ruined. She didn't realize it until after she finished the interview and used the bathroom. She wishes she knew how the weather was and didn't put on makeup.
